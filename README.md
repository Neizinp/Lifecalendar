# Your Life in Weeks

A beautiful, interactive visualization of your life in weeks. Each square represents one week of a 90-year lifespan.

![Preview](preview.png)

## Features

- **90-Year Life Grid** – 52 weeks × 90 years visualization
- **Current Year View** – Daily progress with each dot representing one day
- **Interactive Tooltips** – Hover for details on any week or day
- **LocalStorage** – Your birthdate is remembered
- **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- **Dark Theme** – Modern aesthetic with smooth animations

## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in a browser
2. Enter your birth date
3. Click "Visualize" to see your life in weeks

## Inspiration

Inspired by [Tim Urban's "Your Life in Weeks"](https://waitbutwhy.com/2014/05/life-weeks.html) and [Bryan Braun's implementation](https://www.bryanbraun.com/your-life/).
